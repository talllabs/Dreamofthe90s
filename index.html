The Vibe

Mia & Ashleigh are hosting a slow, playful 90s-kid summer — the kind where you come home with dirt under your fingernails and lots of stories to tell.

We're keeping it small: 8 kids total so we can be mobile and actually go places — parks, libraries, hikes, bowling alleys, beaches.

We love having kiddos who already know each other, so they can keep building memories with friends all summer long.
